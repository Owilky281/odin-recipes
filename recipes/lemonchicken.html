Lemon Chicken

This delicious and easy dish is a care free and relaxing meal to make for any day of the week!

It's as simple as simmer, spice, and set in the slow cooker until it's juicy and done!

All you'll need are the following ingredients:

  • 1 chicken breast per person in the family
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Poultry spices such as thyme, parsley, rosemary, and oregano
  • 1/2 cup of squeezed lemon juice
  • 2 to 3 chopped lemons

Now Let's Dive Into The Dish!

To ensure the very best Lemon Chicken, be sure to do as follows:

  1. Season the raw chicken breast with salt and Pepper
  2. Heat up oil, preferably olive oil, on medium high in a pan
  3. Place the chicken breasts into the pan until both sides are browned, but not cooked through
  4. Remove the browned chicken breasts and place into a slow cooker pot
  5. Pour your 1/2 cup of squeezed lemon, and another 1/2 cup of water
  6. Season with poultry spices until the breasts are well covered
  7. Place the chopped lemons on top of the breasts as needed
  8. Put the lid on the slow cooker pot evenly, and set on low for 6 hours

It's now time to play... THE WAITING GAME!

After six long and foodless hours, it's time for you to enjoy!

Garnish with fresh chopped parsley. Side with rice, pasta, or mixed greens and enjoy!
