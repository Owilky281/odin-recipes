  • Bread. Any bread. Just not mouldy bread.
  • Cheese. Swiss. Marble. Anything that doesn't crumble.
  • Butter. Margarine. As long as it turns bread golden brown. Use it.

You honestly should know what to do. But for the love of all that is holy... here is what you do:

  1. Butter one side of each piece of bread. You're not made of money. That's why you're here.
  2. Heat up a pan to medium heat
  3. Placed the buttered side down on the pan. Place the cheese on top.
  4. Place the other buttered side facing up, with the unbuttered side lying on top of the cheese.
  5. Please tell me you got the previous instruction right. You did, right?
  6. Cook until it looks like this. Or don't. Whatever...
