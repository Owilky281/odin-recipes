It's Time for Some Sweet Creamy Bacon Cabonara!

This is a tried, tested, and true recipe that makes for an easy and delicious meal all year round!

It's the meal to impress your friends, make your mama proud, and win the love of a fair maiden!

But enough banter. Here's what you need:

  • Penne or Angel's hair pasta
  • 1b cooked bacon
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1/2 cup of cream
  • 1 egg
  • Grated parmesan
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • Parsley to garnish, if you want

No time for some sob story about where this recipe comes from. Here's what you do:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the egg, parmesan, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add your pasta to a pot of boiling water for 7-10 minutes. Check for soft consistency!
  3. Mix your butter and cream in a pan on low heat and mix while simmering
  4. Once the butter and cream have thickened up, so that your spoon remains covered in sauce, add the pasta
  5. Add the egg parmesan mix and cooked bacon
  6. Stir your mixture until a nice thick and cream consistency. Add a little water if you need to thin it out
  7. Let it sit for 2-3 minutes for the flavour to settle in

    8. And now it's time to enjoy. Be sure to make the appropriate gestures beforehand!
