It's Time For Some Easy Cheesy Tortellini!

It's a hearty cheesy dish that will simply melt your mind into a dairy based nirvana.

So buckle up, buttercup, because it's time to add a solid recipe notch to your cullinary belt!

Let's get started on what you'll neeed:

\
  • 1 lb of ground beef
  • 1 500mg package of cream cheese
  • 1 750mg package of three cheese tortellini
  • 300g of grated parmesan
  • 300g of mozarella pizza cheese
  • 1 750ml jar of Classico Tomato and Sweet Basil Marinara Sauce
  • Salt, pepper, paprika, basil, and oregano
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

We'll need all of the above to combine beautifully into this:

Now on to what to do:

  1. Start by cooking up your ground beef in a medium to high heat oiled pan. Be sure to drain the grease
  2. Boil your tortellini for about 7-10 minutes, or until the consistency becomes soft and smooth.Strain when done
  3. Remove your ground beef into a bowl. Add the cream cheese and sauce into the pan and stir. Stir until it looks like this:
  4. Add the ground beef, tortellini, and parmesan into the mixture and mix together for 5 minutes, or until the parmesan has melted in well
  5. Pour the mix into a casserole dish. Cover with mozarella pizza cheese
  6. Bake in your oven at 350 degrees for 20-25 minutes
  7. let it sit for 3-5 minutes. Garnish with parsley and serve!
